How it works

A coach that knows your dog — not just dogs.

Tell Laeli about your dog once. From then on, every plan and answer is shaped by their age, breed, history, and what you logged yesterday.

Ask anything, anytime

Biting, potty accidents, leash pulling, a 2am "is this normal?" — get a calm, specific answer instead of a forum rabbit hole.

A real AI coach

Not a generic chatbot. Laeli is real AI trained on force-free, expert-backed methods — and it actually knows your dog's age, breed, and history.

A real daily plan

Short, doable training tasks every day — honest day-by-day programs, never "fix it in a weekend" promises.

A journal that learns

Log wins and setbacks in seconds. Laeli spots patterns — like an evening biting cluster — and adapts the coaching.

Care that never slips

Vaccines, meds, vet visits, grooming, feeding — gentle reminders keep the whole routine on track, so nothing important slips your mind.

Safe by design

Medical, aggression, and severe-anxiety cases are routed to your vet or a certified behaviorist — never improvised.

Built for real life

From first walk to grey muzzle.

  • Every life stage. Puppy basics, adopted-adult transitions, rescue trust-building, and senior, mobility-aware routines.
  • Multiple dogs. Keep a separate profile, plan, and journal for each dog in the house.
  • Care reminders. Medication, vet visits, and routines — gentle nudges so nothing slips.

Why Laeli

The honest one. Real training, not promises.

Most apps sell shortcuts. Laeli sticks to positive-reinforcement, force-free methods grounded in how dogs actually learn — across puppy, adopted adult, rescue, and senior life stages. It tells you the truth about timelines, refuses aversive "quick fixes," and knows when to hand you off to a professional. That's the whole point.
